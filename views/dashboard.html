Hello!

Welcome to our CIS 550 project, designed to match you with the perfect dog based on your preferences. If you want to adopt a dog, we'll show you a series of photos and let you decide if you like or dislike the dogs in the photos. Based on this swiping data, our algorithms will intelligently show you more dog photos, and, at the end, give you information about the perfect dog for you to adopt.

Click "Tinder" to get started on finding the perfect dog for you.

If you already have a dog, click "Breed Guesser" and we'll guess which breed you have.
